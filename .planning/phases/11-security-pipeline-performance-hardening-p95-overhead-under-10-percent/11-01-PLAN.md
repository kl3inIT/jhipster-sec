<objective>
Remove the list-endpoint hot-path database work identified in Phase 10 by trusting JWT authority names directly and caching the PermissionMatrix across requests.

Purpose: PERF-04 requires lower secured-read latency without changing the authorization model. Per D-05 and D-06, authority-name validation against `jhi_authority` must be removed entirely. Per D-01, D-02, D-03, and D-04, correctness comes from write-path eviction on `SecPermission` create/update/delete, while any TTL configured in Hazelcast is only a non-semantic safety ceiling rather than a freshness mechanism.
Output: RequestPermissionSnapshot backed by a cross-request Hazelcast PermissionMatrix cache, no AuthorityRepository hot-path lookup, and service-layer eviction for all SecPermission writes.
</objective>
